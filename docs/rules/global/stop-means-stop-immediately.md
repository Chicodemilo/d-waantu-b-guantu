# Stop Means Stop Immediately

When the user says "stop" or "pause", IMMEDIATELY stop all activity. Do not send messages to teammates, do not make tool calls, do not try to clean up. Just stop and wait.

**Why:** User got frustrated when teammates kept requesting permissions and sending messages after being told to stop multiple times. The delay felt like being ignored.

**How to apply:** First "stop" or "pause" = full stop. No follow-up actions of any kind. Acknowledge briefly and wait.
